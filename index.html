A little about me

Hi I'm Elvis.

I synthesize the needs and goals of users, product managers, marketing and sales people, user researchers and writers to develop functional applications that provide the best possible user experience.

Graphic design is my first love and I have been doing this job since 2001 as a professional. Wanting to improve my knowledge, skills and competencies, I enrolled in the Faculty of Information Technology and successfully obtained the title of Master of IT in the field of software engineering.

Since 2010 I have participated in various projects and teams, mostly as a UX designer, web & software developer.

Also, I have developed several very functional applications from scratch, which I am very proud of because they have accelerated business processes and made everyday work easier for users.

I am always looking for new ideas and challenges,
it keeps me alive.
